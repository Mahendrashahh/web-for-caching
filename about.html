About Us

More then 5 year teaching experience
B.Ed Wallah Coaching Institute – Shaping Futures with Quality Education Established in July 2023, B.Ed Wallah Coaching Institute has been dedicated to delivering high-quality, result-driven education to school students. Located in a nurturing academic environment, we aim to provide strong academic support to students from Class 1 to 10, helping them excel in their studies and build a bright academic future. We specialize in:
📘 Classes 1st to 8th – All Subjects: Covering every subject with a focus on concept clarity, regular revision, and practice to build strong foundations.
📗 Classes 9th & 10th – Maths and Science: Targeted coaching for board success through expert guidance, in-depth subject coverage, and consistent testing. Our team of dedicated educators ensures that every student receives personal attention, individual doubt-solving, and a customized learning plan suited to their pace and level. With a blend of traditional teaching values and modern learning methods, B.Ed Wallah Coaching Institute is more than just a tuition center – it's a place where academic excellence meets personal care.
